Systems, Software, and Snapper

As one of the nation's leading importers of fresh and frozen seafood, Beaver Street Fisheries needed a technology partner that could help them improve both technically and organizationally. We have helped them deliver effective software solutions while simultaneously improving their organizational, managerial, and technical patterns, practices, processes, and systems.

While we have worked with Beaver Street Fisheries over the last decade to develop various custom software solutions to manage inventory, sales, purchasing, logistics, and production, we most recently worked with them to automate a complicated procurement process that continuously supplies their customers with fresh seafood. This was no small task and much of the success on the project can be attributed to the close collaboration between our team and theirs through the use of Software Teaming.

Profound Collective hopes to continue bringing joy to our work with Beaver Street Fisheries and to help them stay ahead of the curve in the seafood industry. We are proud to be their technology partner and look forward to helping them grow and succeed in the future.
