Keep it Simple

Our system is comprised of three main phases: Discovery, Design, and Delivery.

Each phase contains specific activities focused on refining understanding and ensuring alignment with your unique needs.

At the end of each phase you can expect value delivered in the form of artifacts or outcomes that feed into the next steps in the process.

Core to it all are communication, collaboration, and a focus on continuous learning. Without learning, we cannot improve. Without improvement, we cannot deliver value.

Overview

Discovery is about understanding your business deeply before we write a line of code.

We learn about the behaviors and operations that the software will replicate or enhance, forming a detailed picture of how it can best serve your organization.

Our Approach

Our system of delivery is designed to bring clarity and confidence to custom software development, making each phase purposeful and aligned with your unique needs. We start with understanding your business deeply before we write a line of code. Once we have a mutual understanding or a shared vision, we move into delivery. Maintaining and refining your software for the long term, our approach ensures lasting value.

  1. 1. Achieving Mutual Understanding The journey begins with a shared understanding of your business, its goals, and the people who use the software. This phase is critical: we learn about the behaviors and operations that the software will replicate or enhance, forming a detailed picture of how it can best serve your organization. This mutual understanding establishes the foundation upon which all other phases are built, setting the direction for meaningful design and impactful development.
  2. 2. Design Once we’re aligned on your needs, we transition into the Design phase, refining and organizing the insights from the Understanding phase. Here, we work collaboratively to define the software’s features and user interactions. Key artifacts like user flow diagrams, wireframes, and a foundational architecture are produced, offering a clear blueprint for the software’s functionality and user experience. This phase translates ideas into actionable plans, readying the project for efficient and informed development.
  3. 3. Delivery With design plans in hand, we move into Delivery, the phase where the software itself is brought to life. Our team writes the code, continually seeks feedback, and adapts as needed to maintain alignment with your evolving understanding and priorities. This iterative approach lets us refine our understanding and improve the design with each development cycle. By engaging with your team throughout the process, we ensure that the software remains in step with your needs and changes in the business environment.
  4. 4. Maintenance After launch, the focus shifts to Maintenance, where we support and evolve your software to stay valuable over time. We employ four types of maintenance:
    • Corrective: Fixing any issues that arise.
    • Adaptive: Adjusting the software to changes in the operating environment.
    • Preventive: Anticipating potential issues to prevent future disruptions.
    • Perfective: Enhancing performance and features to optimize the software’s value.

    This phase ensures that the software remains secure, efficient, and perfectly tuned to your needs well beyond its initial release.

  5. 5. Optional: Research & Development For clients looking to explore new possibilities, our Research & Development (R&D) cycles offer a flexible way to experiment with new ideas. R&D can happen alongside regular development or independently, creating a sandbox for innovation that can lead to future features, enhancements, or even entirely new software products. This optional phase opens up new avenues, allowing your business to stay on the cutting edge of technology.

Each phase in our approach is crafted to provide clarity, direction, and continuous alignment, ensuring that your software is not only custom-built but custom-sustained for the future. Let’s work together to build something that truly fits your vision and supports your business at every stage.
